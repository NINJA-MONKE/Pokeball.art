Pokeball

  • Start with a circle
  • Make a circle in the middle of that one
  • draw 2 lines from the circle to the edge
  • color top half red

    • Greatball

  • start with circle
  • make circle in middle of that one
  • draw 2 lines from the center circle to edge
  • make red rectangles from top left and right corner to half way to the center
  • color rest of top blue

    • Ultraball

  • start with circle
  • make circle in middle of that one
  • draw 2 lines from the center circle to edge
  • make yellow rectangles at top left and right corner to lines
  • color rest of top black

    • Masterball

  • Start with a circle
  • make circle in the middle of that one
  • draw semi-circles in top right and left corners
  • draw 2 lines from the center circle to the edge
  • color semi-circles red
  • draw rest of top half purple
